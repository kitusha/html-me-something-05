My Personal Story

My name is Kitusha Born and raised in Kenya, East Africa. I come from a family of six with four siblings. Sadly, my father passed away in 2004 from Diabetes. He discovered his condition at a very crucial stage, but it was too late to manage it effectively. Losing him at such an early age was heartbreaking, and I still miss him dearly. However, life goes on, and I carry his memory in my heart as I continue my journey.

Places I Have Visited

In Kenya, I have had the opportunity to visit some incredible places. I've explored game parks teeming with wildlife, vibrant cities, majestic waterfalls, and fascinating caves. Kenya is renowned for its tourism attractions, and its people have excelled in sports, particularly in marathon running and short distance events.

Short History About Kenya

Kenya is located in East Africa and boasts diverse terrain, ranging from a low coastal plain along the Indian Ocean to mountains and plateaus at its center. Nairobi, the capital, sits at an altitude of 5,500 feet (1,700 meters) and is a bustling city in the highlands.

Kenya is home to numerous tourist attractions, including Amboseli National Park, Masai Mara National Reserve, the Great Wildebeest Migration, Lake Nakuru National Park, and more. Its breathtaking landscapes and wildlife draw visitors from all over the world.

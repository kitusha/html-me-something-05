KITUSHA BIOGRAPHY

Introduction

My name is Kitusha. I was born and raised in Kenya, East Africa, in a family of six with four siblings. My mother and dad were married until my dad passed away in 2004 from Diabetes. My dad discovered he had diabetes at a very crucial stage, so he tried to manage it, but it was very late, which took his life. He died at an early age, and I still miss him, but life has to go on anyways.

PLACES I HAVE VISITED

In Kenya, I have visited game parks, cities, waterfalls, and caves. Kenya is known for tourism and sports like running marathons and short runs.

SHORT HISTORY ABOUT KENYA

Kenya is located in East Africa. Its terrain rises from a low coastal plain on the Indian Ocean to mountains and plateaus at its center. Most Kenyans live in the highlands, where Nairobi, the capital, sits at an altitude of 5,500 feet (1,700 meters). Learn more about Kenya on National Geographic Kids
